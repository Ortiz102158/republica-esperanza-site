■ ABOUT US

The Movement for the Salvation of the Republic (MSR) emerges in response to the moral, political, and economic crisis suffocating our people.

Inspired by hostos, the Restorers, and 20-th century popular struggles, we aim to build a Republic of justice, sovereignty, and human dignity.
